Target Audience

The target audience are key stakeholders in the Utah business community, and they have unique needs and interests when it comes to innovation and economic growth and include the entrepreneurs and investors

Personas

Local Residents: Individuals living in Carlsbad, California, and the surrounding coastal areas who are interested in purchasing fresh, locally grown organic foods and supporting a small business.

Health Conscious Individuals: People who prioritize healthy eating and are looking for organic, made-to-order food options, including fresh fruits, produce, fruit drinks, and fruit bags.

Tourist and Visitors: Individuals visiting the coastal areas of Los Angeles to San Diego who are seeking convenient and healthy food options during their stay.

Online Shoppers: Customers who prefer the convenience of online ordering and delivery services for their grocery needs, including those who may not have access to the physical store or food trucks.

Catering and Event Planners: Businesses or individuals looking to cater events or utilize food truck services for their gatherings or functions.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1
    Can Sarah, a busy professional, visit the Bountiful Foods website and place an order for a variety of organic fruits and a fruit bag, select the delivery option and provide her address, then have the order successfully processed, receive her fresh and healthy groceries at her doorstep the next day?
  • Scenario 2
    The Bountiful Foods team plans a food truck event at a local beach. They update the website with the event details, including the date, time, and location. Visitors to the website can see the upcoming event and mark their calendars to enjoy delicious organic food from the Bountiful Foods food truck.
  • Scenario 3
    A family from out of town is visiting the coastal areas of California. They stumble upon the Bountiful Foods website while searching for organic food options nearby. The website provides information about the Carlsbad store and food trucks, allowing the family to find a convenient location to purchase fresh fruits and explore the local food scene.
  • Scenario 4
    Mark, an event planner, is organizing a corporate gathering and is interested in offering organic and healthy food options. He visits the Bountiful Foods website and finds a dedicated section for catering services. Mark fills out the inquiry form with event details and dietary preferences. Bountiful Foods promptly responds, providing a customized catering package for the event.
  • Scenario 5
    It's the peak season for a particular fruit or vegetable grown locally. Bountiful Foods decides to run a promotion to highlight the availability of this fresh produce. The website showcases vibrant images and information about the seasonal item, encouraging visitors to explore recipes and benefits associated with the fruit or vegetable while offering a special discount for purchases.
