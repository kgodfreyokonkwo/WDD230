Discover Point Reyes

Nestled within the pristine wilderness of Point Reyes National Seashore, Alamere Falls presents a captivating natural spectacle. Tucked away along the scenic coastal cliffs, this enchanting waterfall cascades gracefully down the rugged rocks, creating a mesmerizing sight where freshwater meets the salty embrace of the Pacific Ocean.

The pristine waters of Alamere Falls plunge in a graceful manner, creating a gentle mist that mingles with the ocean breeze. Surrounded by the untouched beauty of the national seashore, the falls invite visitors to pause, marvel, and contemplate the magnificent forces of nature at work.
